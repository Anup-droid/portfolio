Hello! It's nice to meet you

I am a biostatistician and work as a Manager – Evidence Synthesis at Novartis in Hyderabad, India. I specialize in Evidence synthesis, Health Economics & Outcomes Research (HEOR), and advanced biostatistical methodologies, focusing on generating comparative data to support global drug submissions.

My core research and operational work centers on Indirect Treatment Comparisons (ITC). I lead the design and execution of quantitative analyses involving Network Meta-Analysis (NMA), Bayesian NMA, and population-adjusted methods like Matching-Adjusted Indirect Comparisons (MAIC) and Simulated Treatment Comparisons (STC). I also apply sophisticated statistical frameworks such as Meta-regression and Multilevel Network Meta-Regression (MLNMR) to resolve clinical trial heterogeneities and bridge evidence gaps.

I hold a Master of Biostatistics and Demography (MBD) from the International Institute for Population Sciences (IIPS), Mumbai (Batch 2018–2020), where I developed a strong foundation in statistical modeling and computational statistics. An avid practitioner of the R programming, I develop interactive R Shiny dashboards to dynamically present and test evidence models, helping clinical and commercial teams make data-driven decisions. I design and implement reproducible analytical workflows, develop R packages. I also leverage Quarto for automated reporting and maintain collaborative project pipelines through GitLab repository management.
